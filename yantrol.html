Application areas:

YANTROL grades are general purpose lubricants for ‘once through’ application situation in textile mills, paper mills and machine tools.

The grades are used in steel mills.

Performance benefits:

  • Excellent oil film strength
  • Very good oiliness
  • Very good of ‘once through’ application and ‘boundary lubrication

Special Features

YANTROL grades are high quality lubricating oils intended for general lubrication of industrial machinery. They are manufactured from selected base stocks and fortified with additives like antiwear and antirust. They are specially developed for steel mill general purpose lubrication.
